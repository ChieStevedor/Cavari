Handling specification support, architect relationships, and end-to-end project coordination — so exceptional manufacturers can focus on the craft.

Why We Exist

The North American lighting
market is broken for
exceptional brands.

Extraordinary European lighting manufacturers — studios with decades of craft, collections that define interiors in Milan and Copenhagen — are invisible in New York and Los Angeles. Not because the market doesn't want them. Because no one has built the right bridge.

The current distribution model treats lighting like commodity hardware. We treat it like the cultural object it is.

Most distributors sign thirty brands and serve none of them well. We will carry a dozen — and be genuinely expert in each one.

Architects specify what they trust. Trust is built through relationships, not catalogues. We are in the room.
